Director’s Message


Education is integral for the growth and development of an individual. The expectation from a coaching institute is always about making the society better for all and to bring out one's true potential in the service of the mankind.

We always believe that excellence in education can make a difference in a person's world.

We would like to express our gratitude for the faith and interest you have shown in New Cristal Academy. Our dreams have been woven with rewarding careers of our students. We strive to accomplish this by supporting them in their efforts through contemporary academic programs, qualified faculties and efficient administration. No false promises.

“Your dreams are our inspiration and your destination is our goal”.

We ensure that no stone will remain unturned in the endeavour to ensure you a great career ahead.
Our simple promise: “WE WILL TEACH YOU WELL”.
Wishing you a very bright future…


Mrs. Devi Suresh
Director-Academics
