One Year Theory cum Entrance

Target Group Current Class XII Students
Mode of Admission Direct admission on percentage basis (above 70% only)
Course Schedule Stage 1: From first week of April till April last (6 days a week)
Stage 2: From May 1st to September 30th.
Stage 3: From October 1st to December 31st
Stage 4: From January 1st to 31st
Stage 5: Immediately after the 12th Board Exams in March till the Entrance Exams.
Course Details Stage 1: Entrance topics for plus one will be completed.
Stage 2: The theory topics for Class XII will be taught during this session from Monday to Saturday
Stage 3: The remaining theory topics and complete entrance topics will be completed.
Stage 4: Model Theory Examinations with discussions.
Stage 5: Crash Program with series of Model Examinations and Discussions for various Engineering/Medical Entrance Examinations.
Batch Options Morning Batch: 6.45am to 8.15am (Monday to Saturday)
OR
Evening Batch: 4.45pm to 6.15pm (Monday to Saturday)
Examinations Unit wise subjective/objective examinations as per given schedule.
Practicals All the students joining this course will be provided with free Practical Classes for Physics, Chemistry & Biology during the course period.
Course Material Separate Course Material for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics both for theory and entrance.
Course Fee Details can be had from the Academy Office
