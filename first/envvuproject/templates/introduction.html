Crystal Eduventures offer integrated coaching solutions for aspirants of important competitive exams. Crystal Eduventures began operations in the year 2010 at Palakkad, Kerala as New Cristal Academy. The institute proved to be a boon for local students who at that time had to travel to neighbouring districts for quality entrance coaching. The institute is the brainchild of Mrs Devi Suresh and Dr. E Krishnadas who brought in his decade long experience as faculty with Prof. P C Thomas Classes, Thrissur, Keralaâ€™s pioneering entrance coaching institute.

From year one, the institute was able to churn out medical and engineering qualifiers. With each passing year the institute lured more and more aspirants to it with its excellent results attributed to excellent coaching methodology, a team of expert faculty, state of the art infrastructure, well stacked library and conveyance for the aspirants. Cristalites became a regular enrollment in every leading medical and engineering colleges across Kerala. This institute is the only of its kind in Palakkad to produce JEE ADVANCED(IIT) qualifiers for successive years. Much of its success is attributed to the extensive use of technology in Coaching.
